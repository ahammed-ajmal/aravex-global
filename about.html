Our Company

Aravex Global Contracting and General Transportation LLC SPC is a UAE-based company headquartered in Mussafah, Abu Dhabi. Established in 2026, we specialize in staff transportation, labor transport, and contracting support services. We are committed to safety, reliability, and efficiency in every operation. Our experienced team and well-maintained fleet allow us to serve corporate, government, and individual clients across the UAE.
